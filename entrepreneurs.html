How it works (end-to-end) +

  1. Join (R1,000/year) → get marketplace access, listing tools, and seller dashboard.
  2. List your product/service → set retail price and commission % (20–30% recommended).
  3. Upload marketing assets → images, descriptions, brand rules, FAQs, delivery/return policy.
  4. Brief ambassadors → we publish your product to the sales force; run promos and incentives.
  5. Sell & fulfill → ship/fulfill on time; commissions tracked; payouts run weekly.
  6. Iterate → optimize price, bundles, cross-sells, and campaigns.
