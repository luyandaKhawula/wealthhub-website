What is a Brand Ambassador?

A MyWealth Hub Brand Ambassador is a trusted advocate who connects quality products and services with their personal network. As an ambassador, you leverage your existing relationships, social media presence, and community connections to promote carefully vetted offerings that provide real value to South African consumers.

This isn't about aggressive selling or compromising your relationships. Instead, you become a bridge between excellent businesses and people who genuinely need their solutions. Every recommendation you make is backed by our quality guarantee, comprehensive training, and ongoing support system. Your success is built on trust, authenticity, and the satisfaction of helping others while building your own financial future.

Stokvel Tax-Free Investment Income. Click to Learn More!

The third stream is investment income. MyWealth Hub operates a digital Stokvel, where members save and invest together using Tax-Free Investment (TFI) accounts.

Members can contribute R1,000 to R3,000 per month (up to R36,000/year) and benefit from:

  • Tax-free returns (all growth is exempt from tax).
  • Group discounts (lower policy costs).
  • Better returns than normal bank savings.
  • Referral commissions when you introduce new Stokvel investors.
